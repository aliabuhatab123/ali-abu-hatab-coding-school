HTML, CSS, and JavaScript Course: Build Professional Websites

The HTML, CSS, and JavaScript course at Ali Abu Hatab Academy is your gateway to web development. Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your skills, this course teaches you how to create interactive and visually appealing websites from scratch. Available online for students in Palestine, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, and Jordan, with a certified completion certificate.

Why Choose This Course?

In today’s job market, web development skills are in high demand. This course equips you with practical knowledge to build modern websites, focusing on real-world projects that align with industry needs. Course Benefits

This course is ideal for beginners eager to enter web development and programmers seeking to improve their HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills. Whether you’re a student, professional, or freelancer, this course provides the knowledge and tools to achieve your goals.
